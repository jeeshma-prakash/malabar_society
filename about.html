About Us

Welcome to The Malabar Multistate Agro Co-operative Society Ltd.

    Established in 2019 under the regulatory framework of the Multistate Cooperative Societies Act of 2002, The Malabar Multistate Agro Co-operative Society Ltd. Has emerged as a leading cooperative entity committed to enhancing the agricultural sector in India. With its headquarters located in the scenic city of Kannur, the society operates under the registration number MSCS/CR/1292/2019, symbolizing its legal standing and commitment to governance.

  • Core focus: support and upliftment of farmers through inputs, training, finance, and market access.
  • Presence: across Kerala and Karnataka through an expanding branch network.
  • People-first: 100+ professionals with 85% women, championing inclusive growth.
  • Values: innovation, sustainability, community development, and cooperative unity.

Chairman
Dear Members, It is with immense pride and gratitude that I welcome you to our Multi‑State Agro Cooperative Society.
